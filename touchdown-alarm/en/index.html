How It Works

A 5-step process to help you build solid habits

1

Set Target Location

Select and register frequently visited locations like office, gym, or school on the map.

2

Set Alarm Time

Set the alarm time and difficulty level. Weekly repeat settings are also available.

3

Alarm Rings

When the alarm rings at the set time, it's time to leave!

4

Go to Destination

The alarm can only be stopped when you arrive at the designated location. It keeps ringing until you get there.

5

Success & Reward

The alarm stops when you arrive at your destination and you earn points. Small successes create big changes!
