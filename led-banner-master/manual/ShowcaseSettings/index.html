Showcase Settings

Components

Showcase Editor

  • Located at the top.
  • Allows you to adjust the size and position of banners.
  • Drag the center of a selected banner to move its position.
  • Drag the edges/corners of a selected banner to adjust its size.
  • Banners can only be selected from the In Use list.

Available

  • List of available banners.
  • Use the right arrow to send a banner to the In Use list.
  • Banners moved to the In Use list are automatically selected.

In Use

  • List of currently used banners.
  • Use the left arrow to send a banner back to the Available list.
  • Tap a banner to select/deselect it.
  • Drag the move button on the right to adjust display priority.
  • The display order in the list corresponds to the display order in the Showcase:
    • The banner at the top of the list appears at the top of the Showcase.
    • The banner at the bottom of the list appears at the bottom of the Showcase.
